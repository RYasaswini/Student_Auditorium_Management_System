Indian classical dance is a umbrella term for various performance arts rooted in Hindu musical theater .Indian classical dancing is very joyful and celebrated thing for people to do in a indian culture. The style of indian classical dancing is very vibrant and motivational.It is a style od dance that is like communication with the gods.The dancer takes the role of the character that they hear in the song or sound and become emotionally connected with the story and audience.

"NATARAJA" is also known as ADALVALLAN is a depiction of the HinduGod Shiva as the divine cosmic dancer.His dance is called the "tandava".The Sculpture is symbolic of shiva as the lord of dance and dramatic arts ,with its style and proportions made according to Hindu texts on arts.

India has many dances,coming from every state in the country.The folk dances of india are much more than mere body movements,form the very ancient times the classical dance froms of india is considered as a discipline and a way to devote yourself to God through art.
